FAQ

Common Questions.

How much does a website cost? +
Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of the project. A standard business website typically starts at ₹8,000–₹20,000. Portfolio sites and landing pages can be more affordable. We offer custom quotes after a free consultation — reach out to get an accurate estimate for your project.
How long does it take to build a website? +
Most projects are completed within 1–3 weeks depending on complexity. Simple landing pages can be ready in as little as 3–5 days. We always provide a clear timeline before starting so you know exactly when to expect your site.
What do I need to provide to get started? +
Generally, we need your brand assets (logo, colors, fonts if you have them), the content you want on the site (text, images), and a clear idea of your goals. Don't worry if you're starting from scratch — we can help with content structure and design direction too.
Will my website be mobile-friendly? +
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully responsive — meaning it looks and works great on all screen sizes, from smartphones to desktop monitors. Mobile optimization is a non-negotiable part of our development process.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch? +
Yes. We offer post-launch support and maintenance packages. Whether it's a content update, a new feature, or a bug fix, we're here to help. We also provide a short handover session so you can manage basic updates yourself if you prefer.
Can you work with existing websites / redesigns? +
Yes, we handle redesigns and improvements to existing sites regularly. Whether it's a full overhaul or targeted improvements to specific pages, we can audit your current site and propose a plan that maximizes impact for your budget.
